Privacy Policy

What information does this site collect?

If you choose to provide your email address when signing up for Elle Otero's newsletter, this site will record your email address and any other information you provide. You may unsubscribe at any time. This website may also collect information about visitors, such as browser name, means of connection to the site, and other such information. This site may also use “cookies” to enhance the visitor experience.

How is this information used and shared?

If you provide your email to subscribe to the newsletter, you will receive the newsletter periodically. Your email will never be sold or shared with spammers, and you will not be subscribed to any other services. If you are a minor, you must have the consent of your parent or guardian to subscribe to the newsletter.
