About the author

Elle Otero grew up on the central coast of California and now lives with her husband and daughter in Monterey, where she draws inspiration for her writing. In addition to her Amazon titles, her work has been published in various literary magazines, and she holds a Ph.D. in Instructional Technology Leadership.
