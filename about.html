About Elle Otero

Elle Otero grew up on the central coast of California and now lives with her husband and daughter in Monterey, California, where she draws inspiration for her writing.

In addition to her Amazon titles, Elle's writing has been published in various literary magazines. She holds a Ph.D. in Instructional Technology Leadership and is a professor at San José State University, and when she's not teaching, researching, or writing, she enjoys spending time in her garden and at the beach with her family.

She welcomes comments from her readers through social media, and you can subscribe to the newsletter for regular updates and freebies. And if you haven't already, jump in and read her work now on Amazon — it's free with Kindle Unlimited! Browse her titles (opens in new tab).
